Santa Clara University prides itself on its sustainability initiatives, such as compostables, recycling, and use of clean energy.

One of the resources that many students are not often aware of consuming is water, especially on campus where students pay an all-inclusive fee rather than paying for utilities separately.

We hope that through this educational quiz you will able to gain insight into your own water usage habits, and can learn what you, and SCU, can do to waste less.
